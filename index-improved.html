About RFD

Founded as the first institution in Europe dedicated solely to Roma democratic participation, we represent a breakthrough commitment to political empowerment and institutional reform.

Our Approach

We work at the intersection of institutional change and community leadership, creating systemic conditions for Roma political voice while building sustainable community movements.

Institutional

Systemic Change

We partner with governments, EU institutions, and international organizations to embed Roma democratic participation into permanent policy frameworks and institutional structures.

Community

Leadership Building

We support Roma leaders, activists, and civil society organizations to build sustainable, accountable political movements rooted in community priorities.

Our Work

We implement strategic initiatives across policy advocacy, research, and community engagement to advance Roma democratic participation and institutional accountability.

Strategy

Policy Advocacy

Working directly with EU institutions and national governments to strengthen Roma representation and embed democratic participation into institutional frameworks.

Evidence

Research Initiative

Conducting independent, high-quality research on Roma political participation, institutional barriers, and reform pathways across Europe.

Network

Community Network

Connecting and supporting Roma leaders across Europe through knowledge exchange, capacity building, and strategic coordination on shared priorities.

Key Indicators

Our research monitors three core dimensions of Roma democratic participation across Europe.

Participation

Political Voice

Institution

Policy Framework

Assessing institutional commitment to Roma democratic inclusion and institutional accountability measures.

Movement

Civil Society

Measuring Roma civil society capacity, organizational strength, and independent advocacy infrastructure.
